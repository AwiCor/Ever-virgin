Lux Rosarii

Light of the Rosary

The Holy Rosary is an ancient prayer of the Church, woven from the words of Scripture and the meditations of the faithful. To pray it is to walk slowly, with Mary, through the life of her Son — through joy and sorrow, through glory and the light of the Gospel.

Each decade gathers ten Hail Marys around a single mystery from the life of Christ. The words become a quiet rhythm; the heart turns inward. What begins as a recited prayer becomes, over time, a contemplation.
